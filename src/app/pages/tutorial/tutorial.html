Welcome to UTask Application

This is an application to remind you on your homework or your task that you need to complete within certain time period.
Here's the guide on how to use this app.

1. Add a new task

Firstly, click the blue (PLUS) button on your botttom right of your screen to open up the add task menu.

2. Fill your todo details

Here, you will need to fill in the details of your task or your homework.

3. Tadaaa... Your task is recorded

Once yout task has been recorded, you can start do your task and when you are done,
you can just click the delete button
